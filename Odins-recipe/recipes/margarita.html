Frozen Margarita

Refreshing cocktail which can be made in 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 50ml Tequilla
  • 25ml Triple sec (e.g Cointreau)
  • 15ml Sugar syrup
  • Large handful of ice
  • Wedge of lime, to garnish

Steps to take

  1. Put all the ingredients except the lime wedge in a blender and blitz until smooth. Tip into a cocktail, margarita or rocks glass, garnish and serve.
